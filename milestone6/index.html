Gameplay Overview

Race, Drift, and Paint

In Ink & Ignition, every race is a chance to leave your mark on the world. Choose your car, master the tracks, and use your speed to create stunning art through your tire tracks!

Game Lore

The Origins of Ink & Ignition

Set in a dystopian future where creativity is suppressed, Ink & Ignition is the story of rebellious racers who use their skill and flair to fight back against an oppressive regime. Through their tire marks, they paint a future of freedom and expression.

Features

  • Dynamic Racing Tracks: Race through vibrant, ever-changing environments.
  • Creative Tire Mark Art: Leave unique patterns on the track that influence the world around you.
  • Customization: Personalize your car and gear to reflect your style and playstyle.
  • Multiplayer Mode: Race against friends or challenge others online!
  • In-Game Events: Participate in special events to unlock exclusive rewards and challenges.
