Worldbuilding & Lore

The Painted Realms

Ink & Ignition takes place in a vibrant world where colors fuel power, and legendary racers are known as Chromatic Pilots. Cities pulse with neon life, and underground circuits control the fate of districts. Players uncover hidden stories behind each track, rival crews, and their own racer legacy.
