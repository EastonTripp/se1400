Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

The site is an overview of the game "Ink & Ignition," this is a game made in unreal engine 5, The developer team and i are work vigoursouly to make this game really fun. the idea behind it is drifting leaves tire marks that paint the canvas, you're allowed to draw whatever, there will be cool cars you can pick from as well.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

The text content will be written by the development team. The images and videos will be captured directly from the game, showcasing gameplay footage and screenshots. We will also use 3D assets from Sketchfab and other resources found through Google for additional visuals and development-related media.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care because "Ink & Ignition" offers a unique combination of drifting and artistic expression. Players can enjoy the thrill of racing while leaving beautiful tire marks behind, turning the game into a canvas of creativity. The game is built with Unreal Engine 5, ensuring high-quality graphics and smooth gameplay.

Why should a site be built for this?

To give people updates about the game we are building, this will also be used as the store page, when the game is done, this will also be where the beta testing will be posted

What would you like users to do at your site?

Download the game, check the progess, play the beta when its released, and get excited for Ink & Ignition

What is the goal of the site?

To provide users the process behind the making of Ink & Ignition. it will serve as a way to check progess as well.
